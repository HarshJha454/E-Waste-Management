Challenges in E-Waste Management

Understanding the key problems faced in managing electronic waste effectively.

Lack of Awareness

Many people are not aware of the importance of proper e-waste disposal, leading to inappropriate disposal methods that harm the environment.

Complex Recycling Process

E-waste contains various materials that require specialized recycling processes, making it difficult to recycle efficiently and economically.

Informal Sector Handling

A large portion of e-waste is handled by the informal sector, which often lacks the technology and knowledge to safely process the waste.

Lack of Legislation

In many regions, there are insufficient laws and regulations governing the disposal and recycling of e-waste, leading to environmental and health risks.
